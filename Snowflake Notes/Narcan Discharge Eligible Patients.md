Here is an updated SBAR regarding the inpatient naloxone dispensing. Thank you for your support to work with Andy to help pull this data.

We are looking to estimate how many at-risk patients are discharged on average from MGB hospitals over a time period.

Key At-Risk Patient Groups for Discharge Naloxone

- Patients with Opioid Use Disorder (SUD): Anyone in treatment for SUD.
- Recent Overdose Survivors: Patients discharged from the ED or inpatient setting after an opioid overdose
- Patients on High-Dose Opioids: Those receiving prescriptions for pain with high daily morphine equivalents (>50-120 MME/day).
- Patients on Medication for Addiction Treatment: Those starting or using methadone or buprenorphine.
